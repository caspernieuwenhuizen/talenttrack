# TalentTrack

**Player development, on the rails.**
A frontend-first talent management system for a single youth football club.

---

## The problem

Coaches manage player development in spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups and a notebook in the kit bag. Evaluations happen twice a season — if someone remembers. Goals are agreed at the parking lot and forgotten by Tuesday. When a coach leaves, the institutional memory leaves with them. Parents pay for "systematic development" and see vibes.

## The solution

TalentTrack is a WordPress plugin that gives one club one source of truth for player development. Frontend-first dashboards for coaches, players and head of development. Role-aware: players see their own card, coaches see their teams, HJO sees everything. Self-hosted on your own WordPress, GPL-licensed, no SaaS lock-in.

## What it does

- **Evaluations + radar.** Hierarchical Technical / Tactical / Physical / Mental categories with subcategories. Weighted overall rating per age group. Trend lines, radar charts, FIFA-style player cards with team podiums.
- **Sessions + attendance.** Plan training and matches per team, mark attendance pitch-side from a phone, archive last season cleanly.
- **Goals with status flow.** Per-player development goals. Priority + status (active / achieved / missed). Player and coach see the same board.
- **Role-aware access.** Granular view/edit capabilities. Read-only observer role for assistants, board members, scouts. Functional roles map to authorization roles (head coach who also has physio rights = a tickbox).
- **Reports + demo data.** Printable A4 player reports. One-click demo data generator with realistic Dutch names, evaluations, attendance patterns — wipe with one click.

## Who it's for

Single-club youth academies. 50–500 players. Dutch amateur leagues, KNVB structure, O8 through O19. One or two HJO-level coordinators, 10–25 coaches across age groups.

Not for federations. Not for top-flight enterprise pipelines.

## Why it's different

Your data, your hosting, your control. WordPress plugin — install, activate, done. No vendor lock-in. Translatable (Dutch ships in the box). Built mobile-first because that's where coaches actually work. Read-only role for board oversight without compromising security.

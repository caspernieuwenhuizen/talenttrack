Blueprint — interactive prototype

Each position has a numbered circle and three depth slots below it. Click a slot to pick a player from the roster on the left, or drag a player onto a slot. Tier 1/2/3 are visually distinct via the coloured pill on the left of each slot. A player can be picked at any number of slots — the ×N badge next to their name in the roster shows how many times they have been placed. The roster reflects the team selection and can be expanded with guests, cross-team picks, or custom names.

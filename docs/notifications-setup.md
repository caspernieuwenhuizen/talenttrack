# Setting up notifications

TalentTrack can send you push notifications for the things that matter — a new task, a fresh evaluation, a coach update on a goal you're working on. Setup is one tap once you have the app installed.

## Before you start

- Install TalentTrack on your phone first ([iPhone](install-on-iphone.md) · [Android](install-on-android.md)).
- Make sure your phone has push notifications enabled at the system level. If you've disabled notifications globally, the in-app prompt won't show.

## Turn on notifications

1. Open TalentTrack from your home screen (not in a browser tab).
2. The first time the app loads, tap **Enable notifications** in the banner near the top.
3. Confirm **Allow** when your phone asks.

That's it. Your phone is now subscribed and the next thing your coach sends will land directly on your lock screen.

## What you'll receive

The kinds of pushes TalentTrack sends:

- New task assigned to you (e.g. "Self-evaluation due Friday").
- Coach replied on one of your goals.
- New evaluation posted on your profile.
- Match or training session moved or cancelled.

You won't be spammed — TalentTrack never sends marketing or promotional pushes. Each notification is a real action you can take.

## Phone number verification

When you accept the first push, TalentTrack records that the phone number on your profile is yours. No SMS code, no extra step.

## Turning notifications off

You can turn pushes off any time:

- **In the app** — Account → Notifications → toggle off. The next push won't be sent.
- **At the OS level** — your phone's Settings → Notifications → TalentTrack. Useful when you want to silence one device but keep another.

## I can't see "Enable notifications"

Three things to check:

1. **Are you in the installed app, not a browser tab?** Open from the home screen icon.
2. **Did you decline the prompt earlier?** Open your phone's Settings → Notifications → TalentTrack and re-enable. The in-app banner reappears next launch.
3. **Did your account just sign in for the first time?** Sign out and back in once — older browser sessions occasionally hold onto a stale state.

If none of these work, ask your club admin — there's a small chance your account doesn't have a phone number on file yet.

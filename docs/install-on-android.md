# Install TalentTrack on your Android phone

You can install TalentTrack as an app on Android. The app opens fullscreen and can send you notifications about new tasks, evaluations, and goal updates.

## You need

- An Android phone running Chrome (most other browsers also work, but Chrome is the simplest path).

## Steps

1. Open TalentTrack in Chrome and sign in.
2. A small banner usually appears at the bottom: **Add TalentTrack to Home screen**. Tap **Install**.

If you missed the banner:

1. Tap the **⋮** menu (top right of Chrome).
2. Choose **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen** on older Chrome versions).
3. Confirm by tapping **Install**.

A TalentTrack icon appears on your home screen.

## After install

- Open the app and accept **Allow Notifications** when prompted. Without this you won't see push notifications even if your account has them enabled.
- The first push notification you receive verifies the phone number on your TalentTrack account — no SMS code needed.

## Troubleshooting

- **No install option** — your Chrome may be too old. Update from the Play Store and try again.
- **Notifications never arrive** — open Android Settings → Apps → TalentTrack → Notifications and make sure all categories are enabled. Battery optimisation can also delay or drop pushes; mark TalentTrack as **Unrestricted** under battery settings if pushes feel slow.
- **Reinstalling** — long-press the icon, choose **Uninstall**, then repeat the install steps.

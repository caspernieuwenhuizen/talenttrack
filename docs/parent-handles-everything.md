# Parent guide for younger players (U8 – U10)

If your child plays in a U8, U9, or U10 squad, TalentTrack treats you — the parent — as the primary contact. The player record itself doesn't need its own email or phone; everything reaches you instead.

## What that looks like in practice

- The invitation to TalentTrack is sent to **your** email, not the child's.
- Tasks, goal updates, and notifications flow to your account.
- You see your child's profile, evaluations, goals, and upcoming activities — exactly the same view a player would.
- The coach can address your child by name in the app, but the message arrives on your phone.

## Setup

1. Accept the invitation email — that creates your parent account.
2. Install TalentTrack on your phone ([iPhone](install-on-iphone.md) · [Android](install-on-android.md)).
3. Turn on notifications ([Notifications setup](notifications-setup.md)).

After that you'll see "My child" in the dashboard. Tap to open your child's profile.

Your dashboard mirrors exactly what your child sees: the same development tiles, in the same order, relabeled to your child's first name — "Sven's card", "Sven's evaluations", "Sven's development plan", and so on. Whatever the academy switches on or off for players is reflected on your screen too, so you and your child are always looking at the same set of surfaces. Every tile opens your child's record. Your own account settings stay separate — they're not relabeled or duplicated per child.

## When you have multiple children at the academy

A single parent account can be linked to several children. Each child has their own profile under "My children"; switching between them takes one tap. You only need one TalentTrack install and one set of notifications — pushes name the relevant child in the body so you know which one a message is about.

## When your child turns 11 or 12

The contact strategy shifts. From U11 your child can have their own phone account and start receiving their pushes directly. You stay linked as a parent and continue to see everything, but the coach can also reach the player straight on their phone.

When that transition happens:

- Your child gets their own invitation (sent to a phone number you give the academy, not an email).
- They install TalentTrack on their phone, accept notifications, and they're set.
- Your account loses nothing — both surfaces remain active.

## Privacy

Children under 13 are subject to extra safeguards. The full list lives in [Access control](access-control.md), but the headline:

- Only roles with explicit "view player" permission can see your child's record.
- Sensitive notes (medical, family situation) are gated behind a separate capability, audit-logged on every read.
- The academy admin can see who has accessed your child's data and when.

## Asking the academy something

Two paths:

- **In-app conversation** — every goal has a chat thread. Reply there for goal-specific questions.
- **Direct contact** — your child's coach is listed under the team page with email + phone. Use the channel they prefer.
